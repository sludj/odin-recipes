Work Until Seven

Description

The Work Until Seven is a potent, sweet, and ginger heavy drink that helps transition from a late night at work to a relaxing evening at home

Ingredients

  • 2 oz whiskey (bourbon or rye)
  • .75 oz St. Germaine
  • .25 oz lime juice
  • 3 dashes angostura bitters
  • 2 oz strong ginger beer

Steps

  1. Add the juice from one wedge into a glass. Liberally add bitters
  2. Add the whiskey and St. Germaine to the glass
  3. Stir for 45 seconds
  4. Top with ginger beer
