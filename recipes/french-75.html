French 75

Description

The French 75 is a damn good brunch cocktail that only requires a handful of ingredients. Light and crisp, with a good sweet citrus flavor, a crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz gin
  • .75 oz simple syrup
  • .75 oz lemon juice
  • 3 ounces of prosecco

Steps

  1. Combine 1.5 ounces of gin with .75 ounces of simple syrup and .75 ounces of lemon juice
  2. Top with prosecco to taste, about 3 oz
