Old Fashioned

Description

The Old Fashioned is simple, strong, and customizable. Here we stick with the classic recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 oz whiskey (bourbon or rye)
  • .75 oz simple syrup
  • 3 dashes angostura bitters
  • 1 cocktail cherry

Steps

  1. Combine the whiskey with the simple syrup
  2. Liberally dash angostura bitters
  3. Add the cocktail cherry
